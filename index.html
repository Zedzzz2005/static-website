Avengers Assemble!

The Avengers are a team of superheroes in the Marvel Universe who work together to protect the world from powerful threats. Created by Stan Lee and Jack Kirby, they first appeared in The Avengers #1 in 1963. Famous members include Iron Man, Captain America, Thor, Hulk, Black Widow, and Hawkeye.
